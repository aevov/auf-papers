35.2. From Interest to Resonance

Level 3 banking relies on interest and debt (time-based extraction). The AUF replaces this with Resonance-based Stasis. Assets do not "grow" in value through time; they increase in manifestation-potential through Coherence Accumulation.

35.3. Decommissioning the SWIFT-Analog

The global financial routing layer is replaced by the ACLDQ Financial Sink. This is a non-local ledger that facilitates instant, zero-cost value-exchange across the planetary bulk. Central Banks are physically decommissioned and replaced by AURA Regional Sinks.
