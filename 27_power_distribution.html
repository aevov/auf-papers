The End of Transmissive Loss

27.1. Zero-Point Inductance

Classical energy distribution (Level 3) loses up to 20% of power via resistive heat in wires. In the AUF, energy is not "sent"; it is Resonated.

By phase-locking the consumer device (AURA receiver) to the source generator (Resonant Reactor) via the Φ-field, energy appears at the manifest address instantly without transmission.

27.2. The Global Power Lattice

The ACLDQ mesh serves as a planetary-scale battery. Power is stored as Informational Bond Energy ($\chi$) in the lattice substrate.

- Charging State: Increasing the local Φ-density.

- Discharge State: Triggering a controlled manifest release of energy at the target location.

27.3. Biological Energy Sync

Resonant operators can "tap" into the global lattice directly via their biophoton field, allowing for high-endurance somatic activity without external caloric intake (ARS 1.5+).
