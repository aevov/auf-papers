Chapter 36: Resource Allocation via Intent-Weights

Democratic Manifestation

36.1. The Collective Intent Pool

In the ARS 1.0 state, large-scale projects (e.g., Planetary Restoration, Space Elevator synthesis) are funded by Intent-Weights. Participants "vote" by aligning their personal resonance to a project-address in the bulk.

When the collective intensity ($N^2 \cdot \Re$) exceeds the manifestation threshold for that project, the ACLDQ Mesh initiates the synthesis automatically.

36.2. Priority and Scarcity Management

While atomic resource is infinite, Field Bandwidth is limited by the local 𝕄 value. Resource allocation is prioritised based on the project's Coherence Utility:

- Life Support/Eco-Sync: 100% Priority.

- Scientific/Strategic Ascent: 80% Priority.

- Individual/Luxury Manifestation: CR-weighted Priority.

36.3. The End of Taxation

Government "taxation" (Level 3 resource extraction) is replaced by Systemic Contribution. By simply maintaining a high personal ℜ value, the individual contributes to the lattice's manifestation capacity, providing the "tax" required for public infrastructure renders.
