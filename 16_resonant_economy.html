16.2. Coherence Reputation (CR): The Non-Fungible Value

In a world where any material can be rendered, traditional "Money" (Level 3 debt-based scripts) becomes meaningless. Value is redefined as Coherence Reputation (CR)—a quantification of an individual’s or collective’s contribution to the global resonance.
