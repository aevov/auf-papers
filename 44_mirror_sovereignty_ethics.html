Chapter 44: The Ethics of Mirror Sovereignty

Jurisprudence in a Post-Manifest World

44.1. The Sovereign Intent Criterion

In a world where intent is manifest, the "Law" must shift from physical action to Informational Intent.

The AUF defines Harmful Intent as any projection that attempts to Decoherent or De-mirror another observer's SIS without their phase-authorization.

44.2. Manifestation Rights

Every observer has the inherent right to manifest their personal local manifold, provided it does not interfere with the global 𝕄-lattice. Disputes are resolved via Phase-Mediation—the comparison of the two competing intent-vectors in the Φ-bulk.

44.3. The Problem of 'Source-Greed'

The ultimate ethical challenge of Level 4+ occurs when an individual attempts to "Tunnel" into the Source at a rate that causes local field depletion. Chapter 44 establishes the Quadratic Resource Cap to ensure the equitability of manifestation potential across the planetary mesh.
